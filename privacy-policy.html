Privacy Policy for Umrah & Hajj App

Last Updated: August 18, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Umrah & Hajj Guide ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Umrah & Hajj mobile application (the "App"). Our app is a free educational resource designed to provide authentic Islamic guidance for pilgrimage and daily worship. All content has been verified by Islamic scholars and thoroughly reviewed to ensure accuracy and appropriateness for users of all ages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 No Account Required

Our app does not require user registration or account creation. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information.

2.2 Voluntary Feedback Only

The only personal information we collect is when you voluntarily choose to:

  • Submit Feedback: If you send us feedback, suggestions, or report issues, we collect:
    • Your email address (to respond to your feedback)
    • The content of your feedback message
    • Date and time of submission

2.3 Automatically Collected Information (Non-Personal)

  • Device Information: Basic device model and operating system (for app compatibility)
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve performance
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to fix bugs and improve stability

2.4 No Location or Personal Data

  • No GPS/Location Tracking: We do not collect or access your location data
  • No Camera Access: We do not access your camera or photos
  • No Contacts Access: We do not access your contact list or personal files
  • No Social Media Integration: We do not connect to or collect data from social media accounts

3. How We Use Your Information

Minimal Data Collection Approach: Our app is designed to function without collecting personal information.

3.1 Feedback Data Usage

When you voluntarily submit feedback, we use your information to:

  • Respond to your questions, suggestions, or concerns
  • Improve the app based on user feedback
  • Fix reported issues or bugs
  • Enhance educational content quality

3.2 Anonymous Analytics

We use anonymous, non-personal data to:

  • Monitor app performance and stability
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Understand which educational features are most helpful
  • Improve overall user experience

3.3 What We DON'T Do

  • No location-based services (all content works offline)
  • No personalized tracking or user profiling
  • No targeted advertising (the app is completely ad-free)
  • No commercial use of any data
  • No selling or sharing of user information

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

4.2 Extremely Limited Data Sharing

Since we collect minimal data, there is very little to share. We may only disclose information in these rare circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or court order
  • Technical Support: Anonymous crash reports may be shared with app development platforms (Google Play Console) to fix technical issues
  • Security: To prevent app abuse or protect user safety

4.3 Feedback Data Protection

  • Feedback emails are stored securely and encrypted
  • Only accessed by authorized team members to respond to your inquiry
  • Never shared with third parties for any purpose
  • Automatically deleted after 2 years unless ongoing support is needed

5. Data Security

Since we collect minimal personal information, our security approach focuses on protecting the limited data we do handle:

5.1 Feedback Data Protection

  • Encryption: All feedback emails and messages are encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Feedback data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Limited Access: Only authorized team members can access feedback for response purposes
  • Regular Deletion: Feedback data is automatically deleted after a specified retention period

5.2 General Security Measures

  • No User Accounts: No account data to compromise or breach
  • Minimal Data Collection: Less data means fewer security risks
  • Regular Updates: App security is regularly updated and maintained
  • Anonymous Analytics: All usage analytics are completely anonymous and cannot be traced to individuals

6. Your Privacy Rights

Since our app doesn't require accounts or collect extensive personal data, your privacy is protected by design. However, if you have submitted feedback, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any feedback you've submitted
  • Correction: Update or correct any feedback information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your feedback and email address
  • Response: Receive a response to your feedback inquiries

To exercise these rights regarding your feedback, contact us at hajjumrahguide4you@gmail.com.

No Account Management Needed: Since no user accounts are required, there are no account settings to manage or personal profiles to update.

7. Children's Privacy and Family-Friendly Content

This app is safe and appropriate for users of all ages, including children. Our educational content has been carefully reviewed and verified by Islamic scholars to ensure it is suitable for the entire family.

  • For users under 13: Parental consent may be required in some regions. We encourage parents to review this policy with their children.
  • Educational Focus: All content is educational and promotes positive Islamic values and practices.
  • No Inappropriate Content: Our app contains no inappropriate material, advertisements, or external links that could expose children to unsuitable content.
  • Verified Sources: All religious content comes from authenticated Islamic sources and has undergone scholarly review.

If you are a parent or guardian with questions about your child's use of the app, please contact us at hajjumrahguide4you@gmail.com.

8. App Features and Data Usage

Our app provides educational content including:

  • Step-by-step Hajj and Umrah instructions (no location tracking needed)
  • Authentic duas (supplications) with Arabic text, transliteration, and translations
  • Audio recitations of prayers and Quranic verses
  • Educational articles about Islamic rituals and practices
  • Visual guides and illustrations for pilgrimage procedures

Privacy-Friendly Design:

  • All content works completely offline after initial download
  • No internet connection required for core features
  • No location services needed - content is universally applicable
  • No user tracking or behavior monitoring
  • No push notifications or interruptions

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or tracking technologies in our app. Since our app:

  • Works completely offline after initial download
  • Doesn't require user accounts or logins
  • Doesn't connect to external websites or services
  • Doesn't track user behavior or preferences

There are no cookies or tracking mechanisms to manage or disable.

  • No Cloud Services: All content is stored locally on your device

Important: Any anonymous analytics shared with app stores cannot be traced back to individual users and is used solely for technical improvements.
